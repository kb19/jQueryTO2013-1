Darren Rawlings

Concept Artist

Darren, (more commonly known as `Rawls`) began his life and love of art by drawing on the walls of his mothers womb. Not long after that, he was successfully trained as a classical animator and artistic assassin. Currently, Rawls is the creative director and owner of Thinkmore Studios, a company specializing in all things animation, illustration and motion graphics. Thinkmore has animated award winning shows for Discovery Channel, BBC and multiple music videos for Warner Bros. Rawls has just recently finished a graphic novel for Scholastic, due out this summer.

Rene Pardo

Strategic Advisor

Mr. Pardo is a graduate of McGill (BSc 1968) and Harvard (M.Ed., 1968). As a first financier and/or co-founder of high tech start-ups, his initiatives have resulted in well over $75 million of R&D. Mr Pardo co-invented the world's first electronic spreadsheet and the first online educational multimedia game. He also created the first artistic computer animation with Norman McLaren at the National Film Board and developed the world's first online conference registration and messaging system.
